<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/area-of-a-circle/1">Area of a Circle</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Easy</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Given the radius of circle <strong>r</strong>. The task is to find the area of circle.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input:</strong> r = 5</span><br><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Output:</strong> 78.53982</span><br><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Explanation:</strong> As area = π * r * r = π * 5 * 5 = 78.53982</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input:</strong> r = 2</span><br><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Output:</strong> 12.56637</span><br><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Explanation:</strong> As area = π * r * r = π * 2 * 2 = 12.56637</span></pre>
